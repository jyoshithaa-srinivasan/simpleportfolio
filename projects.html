  • Rock Paper Scissors

    March 2024
    Player Input: Allow players to select their choice (rock, paper, or scissors) through clickable buttons or keyboard input. Computer Choice: Simulate the computer's choice randomly to compete against the player. Game Logic: Implement the rules of the game to determine the winner based on the choices made by the player and the computer (e.g., rock crushes scissors, scissors cuts paper, paper covers rock). Display Results: Show the outcome of each round, indicating whether the player wins, loses, or ties with the computer. Score Keeping: Keep track of the player's and computer's scores throughout multiple rounds of play. Reset Button: Allow players to reset the game and start over if they wish to play again.
